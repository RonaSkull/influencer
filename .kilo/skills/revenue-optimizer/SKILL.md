## RPM Optimization Strategies

### Network Selection

**Mediavine** (Current - Most Sites)
- Requirements: 50K sessions/month
- Typical RPM: $15-35
- **Pros:** Reliable, good support, dashboard
- **Cons:** Takes 25% commission, strict requirements

**Mediavine Journey** (Newer Sites)
- Requirements: 10K sessions/month
- Typical RPM: $10-20
- **Opportunity:** Several sites close to eligibility

**AdThrive** (Premium)
- Requirements: 100K pageviews/month
- Typical RPM: $20-45
- **Target:** Hello Hayley, Melrose Family (if traffic recovers)

**Ezoic** (Testing Option)
- Requirements: No minimum
- Typical RPM: $8-25 (varies widely)
- **Use case:** Sites below Mediavine threshold

### RPM Improvement Tactics

**1. Ad Placement Testing**
- In-content ads (highest RPM)
- Sidebar ads (steady performance)
- Header/footer (lower RPM but volume)
- **Test:** Ad density (more ads vs. UX)

**2. Content-Type Optimization**
- Longer content = more ad units
- Commercial intent keywords = higher CPMs
- Seasonal content = Q4 boost
- **Example:** "Best [product]" posts outperform "How to" for ads

**3. Traffic Quality**
- US/Canada/UK/AU traffic = higher CPMs
- Desktop > mobile (typically)
- Engaged users = better ad performance
- **Action:** Optimize for high-value geographic traffic

**4. Seasonal Strategies**
- Q4 (Oct-Dec): CPMs spike 50-100%
- **Strategy:** Push traffic hard in Q4
- **Content:** Publish gift guides, holiday content

## Affiliate Strategy

### High-Converting Products
**Amazon Associates:**
- Best for: Product roundups, "best of" lists
- Commission: 1-10% depending on category
- Cookie: 24 hours
- **Tip:** Link to shopping cart, not individual products (higher conversion)

**ShareASale / Other Networks:**
- Best for: Niche products, higher commissions
- Commission: Often 15-30%
- Cookie: 30-90 days
- **Examples:** Home decor, craft supplies, digital products

### Affiliate Optimization
**1. Strategic Placement**
- First 300 words (readers most engaged)
- Within how-to steps (contextual)
- Comparison tables (easy decision-making)
- **Avoid:** Bottom of post (low clicks)

**2. Link Types**
- Text links (high CTR, natural)
- Image links (visual products)
- Buttons (clear CTA)
- **Test:** Which format converts best per site

**3. Disclosure Compliance**
- FTC requires clear disclosure
- Place disclosure BEFORE affiliate links
- Use clear language ("I earn from qualifying purchases")
- **Legal:** Protects you, builds trust

## Monetization Experiments

### Experiment Framework
**1. Hypothesis**
- "Adding 2 more in-content ads will increase RPM by 15%"

**2. Test Setup**
- Control group (current setup)
- Test group (new setup)
- Run for 2-4 weeks minimum

**3. Measure**
- RPM change
- User engagement change (time on page, bounce rate)
- Overall revenue impact

**4. Decision**
- If revenue up + engagement stable → implement
- If revenue up but engagement down → calculate trade-off
- If revenue flat or down → reject
